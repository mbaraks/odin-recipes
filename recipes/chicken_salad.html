Description

Chicken salad is a light and flavorful dish made by combining cooked, shredded or chopped chicken with various fresh ingredients like lettuce, cucumbers, tomatoes, and onions. The chicken can be grilled, boiled, or roasted, and is often mixed with a dressing such as mayonnaise, yogurt, or a vinaigrette to add creaminess and flavor.

This salad is versatile and can include extras like boiled eggs, avocado, cheese, or nuts for added texture and nutrition. Itâ€™s commonly served chilled, making it a refreshing option for lunch or dinner, and can be eaten on its own, in a sandwich, or wrapped in a tortilla for a quick and healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup blanched slivered almonds
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat
  • 1 rib celery, chopped

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place almonds in a frying pan. Toast over medium-high heat, shaking frequently. Watch carefully, as they burn easily.
  3. Mix together mayonnaise, lemon juice, and pepper in a medium bowl.
  4. Toss with chicken, toasted almonds, and celery.
  5. Enjoy!
